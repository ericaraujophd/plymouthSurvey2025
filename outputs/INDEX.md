## How to Use This Analysis

### For Search Committee Members

**Week 1:**
- Read [Executive Summary](executive_summary.md)
- Review key visualizations: [church_strengths.png](figures/church_strengths.png), [cultural_challenges.png](figures/cultural_challenges.png)
- Discuss initial impressions at committee meeting

**Week 2:**
- Read full [Analysis Report](analysis_report.md) sections 1-6
- Note surprises, confirmations, concerns
- Review worship-related word clouds

**Week 3:**
- Read [Analysis Report](analysis_report.md) sections 7-12
- Review pastor-related word clouds
- Begin drafting profile using [Profile Guidance](profile_guidance.md)

**Week 4:**
- Complete church profile form with [Profile Guidance](profile_guidance.md)
- Cross-reference with church records for missing data
- Review completed draft with full committee

### For Council Members

**30-Minute Overview:**
1. Read [Executive Summary](executive_summary.md) (10 min)
2. Review [pastor_priorities_wordcloud.png](figures/pastor_priorities_wordcloud.png) (2 min)
3. Review [pastor_qualities_wordcloud.png](figures/pastor_qualities_wordcloud.png) (2 min)
4. Read Analysis Report sections: Executive Summary, Red Flags & Cautions (15 min)

**Approve/Discuss:**
- Do findings align with your understanding?
- Any surprises or concerns?
- Ready to move forward with profile completion?

### For Pastoral Candidates (If Shared)

**Read in this order:**
1. [Executive Summary](executive_summary.md) - Get the big picture
2. [Analysis Report](analysis_report.md) - Section 9 (Pastor Search) and Section 6 (Strengths)
3. [church_strengths.png](figures/church_strengths.png) - What they're proud of
4. [cultural_challenges.png](figures/cultural_challenges.png) - What they're concerned about
5. [pastor_priorities_wordcloud.png](figures/pastor_priorities_wordcloud.png) - What they want from you

**Ask yourself:**
- Do I have the gifts they're asking for?
- Can I address their challenges?
- Do their strengths align with my ministry style?
- Am I prepared for the complexity (denominational tension, youth crisis)?

### For Church Profile Form Completion

**Person filling out form should:**

1. Have [church-profile-form.md](../church-profile-form.md) open in one window
2. Have [Profile Guidance](profile_guidance.md) open in another window
3. Keep [Analysis Report](analysis_report.md) handy for reference
4. Have church records available for:
   - Demographics (age percentages, occupation distribution)
   - Financial data (budgets, salaries, benefits)
   - Facility information
   - Recent pastoral history

**Process:**
- Go through profile form section by section
- For each section, consult corresponding guidance section
- Copy/adapt recommended text
- Verify with church records where needed
- Adjust language to council's voice while maintaining data accuracy
