# Executive Summary
## Plymouth Heights CRC Congregational Survey Analysis

**Date:** December 2025

## Quick Overview

This survey analysis reveals a **highly committed, missionally engaged congregation** that values biblical preaching and community presence, while acknowledging significant challenges in youth retention and denominational tensions. - Describe a time you worked through staff conflict
